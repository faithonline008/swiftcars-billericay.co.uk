About

Our Story

In December 2019, my wife and I purchased swift cars. We have lived in Billericay for ten years and are raising two children here, over the years we have fallen in love with the town. When the opportunity came up to buy the company we jumped at the chance.
As a taxi driver myself I was excited to take on the challenge.
In February 2020, we bought a new partner into the Swift Family. Gill had been an employee for many years at Swift and bought with her a wealth of knowledge of both the taxi industry and the local area. Our aim is to provide great service to the town and we always put our customers first. We also want to give back to the community and support other local businesses. We have installed free phones at both the St Andrews health centre and the Billericay town football club, both phones will give people a direct line to the office. We have also partnered with Billericay Cricket club as a shirt sponsor for the next 4 years. This is a very proud moment for us to have our logo on the shirt and to support our local team. We look forward to being to go to taxi company for Billericay and helping our great town become even greater. Louis
